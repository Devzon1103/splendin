Re-imagine your business and design the product as per your business needs.

Data Model Designer

Enterprise level Domain Data Model represents the structure and relationship of data within a specific domain. Domain Data Model comprises standard module-based structures that cater to business needs across various Financial and Non-Financial Industries.

Splendin Designer Platform comes with a standard domain data model for quick setup of business applications and further allows businesses to extend the data model to suit the unique business needs.

Screen Designer

Leverage the intuitive screen designer module to quickly create screens for business applications. Features include User Interface Wireframing & Prototyping for multiple layouts across objects. Role-based access control enables customized views for users or clients across Web and Mobile.

Rule Designer

Enable complex business logic via the rules designer to define, manage, and automate business rules. Businesses can automate rules for products, workflow optimization, and ensure compliance with business policies.

With Rule Designer’s user-friendly interface, design various types of rules such as expression builder, decision table, decision tree, and scorecard.

Workflow Designer

Workflow Designer enables businesses to create and/or organize tasks, define workflow stages, and assign workflows to users/queues. Businesses can track SLAs, manage workflows effectively, and improve efficiency.

Notification Designer

Designer tool that enables businesses to share updates, transaction alerts, system action notifications, and more. Customize notification content and improve user engagement with timely notification alerts.

API Designer

API Designer enables integration with third-party APIs with a quick turnaround time. Businesses can leverage an API-first design approach to smoothly integrate with applications and accelerate time-to-market.

Lending Solutions

Lending product solutions

UPI Credit Line

Explore New Lines of Credit with our “UPI Credit Line” product solution that helps businesses to enable credit on UPI rails. With Splendin Enterprise Product Suite, businesses can set up, access, and provide immediate credit access to their customers on UPI.

Personal Loan

Consumers demand quick and hassle-free lending options from businesses. Splendin Personal Loan solution's in-built features enable simple and easy loan journeys that empower businesses to capture, process, and disburse loans quickly.

Consumer Durable Loan

Consumers are looking for loans to purchase durable products seamlessly in this digital world. Splendin Consumer Durable Loan solution helps businesses quickly assess customer creditworthiness, implement rule-based lending journeys, and enable instant loans.
